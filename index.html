How 2 In 90 Days

Each of us has a dream, a passion, an awesome life awaiting us and within a short amount of time dreams can be realized. Take this NuCamp course as an example, 30 days ago most of us could barely get through a simple CSS and HTML form. In another 60+ days, we’ll be well on our way to living our dreams.

Why 90 Days?

It’s a season/quarter in a year and it’s long enough for whatever change we’ve implemented to be a solid repeatable habit. There are various studies that indicate that in order for an activity/action to be a habit that we need to repeat the habit for a minimum of five times and as many as 28 times depending on all of the surrounding factors. Within 90 days, the new habit will be in place and the transformative results will start showing.

Body building quote:

In two weeks (15 days), you’ll FEEL it. In four weeks (30 days), you’ll SEE it. In twelve weeks (90 days,) you’ll HEAR it. That’s when the “magic happens” You start receiving positive reinforcement from those around you making it that much easier to continue the positive habit that leads to life

Sean Coleman, The Wit and Wisdom of Sean Coleman, 2019
